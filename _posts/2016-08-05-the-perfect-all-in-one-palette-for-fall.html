I know what you’re thinking—Susan, don’t wish Summer away so quickly! 

I could say I’m not trying to, but that might be a lie. It’s been a hot and humid one in the Midwest, and I’m so ready for the cool breeze of Fall. 

I’m bringing this to you in August, however, because now’s your chance to get your hands on your perfect all in one palette to use this fall. This is the Lorac Refined Romance palette, created for this year’s Nordstrom Anniversary Sale. Meaning, I believe you can only get this before August 8th. See why I’m bringing up Fall so early? 

Now, for starters, the packaging of this product is kind of meh for me. I love florals, but I love the sleek, simple looks of Lorac’s other palettes. Getting inside this bad boy, however, makes the cover nothing to think about. You’ve got 6 shadows, in a perfect color combo for fall. A nice shimmery white, a gold shimmer, and a garnet shimmer occupy the top row. Then you have the perfect matte light brown, a satin taupe like color, and a matte garnet shade. I’m pretty much obsessed with these color choices. You may even catch me wearing some more fall inspired looks in August, because I love the colors so much. You can create many different looks, and the combo of matte and shimmer gives you everything you need. 

Moving over to the cheeky side of things, the palette has two highlighters, one a pale champagne color and one a bit pinky, and a beautiful dusty rose matte blush. For starters, this is the blush shade i’m most drawn to in the cooler months, so that’s an instant win. The highlighters, while I am not sure how to wear the pink one just yet, are very pretty as well. They’re not as pigmented as I expected, being Lorac, but they build up really nicely. I think i’ll end up using the pink shade as either eyeshadow or mix it in with the blush shade to create a more luminous flush to the cheeks. 

Formula-wise, the eyeshadows technically aren’t their “Pro” formula. However, I think they’re just as good. Everything is very pigmented as you’d expect and blend easily. The shimmers pack a punch and the mattes aren’t chalky or patchy. 

I can say the same for the blush, formula is nice, color payoff is good, blends well. And since I mentioned the highlight formula above, that covers it! 

This beauty will definitely be traveling with me, and help me cut way down on the amount of products taken on a trip. It’s a great multiple use product, and a hell of a deal! Check it out!
