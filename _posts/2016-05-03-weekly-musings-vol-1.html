I wanted to do a wrap-up style post, but locking into a weekly cadence seemed like I could end up scraping around for things to talk about when I had a rather boring week. Which, let’s be honest, happens quite often. So, the cadence for this series will be much more broad, and i’m thinking at least once a month will be good, or maybe more if there’s a lot going on.
I want this series to be more about what's on my mind in the given time, rather than always have a specific topic. I’d like to share articles I happened upon that I think are worth a read, books i’ve finished or started, my opinion on current events, and other sources of inspiration for my life and my work.

To read: If your workplace uses any sort of instant messaging software for communication, I think you’ll enjoy this article. While it’s a longer read, there are some excellent points on how this can actually diminish the productivity of employees if used poorly—definitely a different perspective than most out there on the topic!

Also, this book has given me a need to bring more blooms into my life. I have a bit of space for planters at the new house, so I'm excited to delve into the world of gardening a bit. Expect photos and posts to come this spring + summer!
