It's a quick one today- wanted to share my really simple makeup routine that's ending up being my beginning of the week, back to work go-to look. 

I start with the base of all bases (that never seems to get enough love in the beauty world), Clinique's Even Better foundation. It's been around for years, and I've been using it this whole time. Sometimes it gets buried in the bottom of the drawer, but when I want a flawless, skin-like, demi-matte finish, I always come back to this one.  

From there I'll sweep a little Benefit Hoola basically all over my face to warm it up, followed by tapping some Becca Shimmering Skin Perfecter in the shade Moonstone over my cheek bones. 

With the eyes I've been grabbing the Bobbi Brown Long Wear Cream Shadow Stick in the shade Sunlight Gold, going all over the lid and heavy on the inner corners to highlight. Then it's a few coats of Maybeline The Rocket mascara to really give the lashes an oomph. 

I finish off the look with one of my favorite pinky nude shades, the revlon lip butter in berry smoothie, and I'm out the door!
