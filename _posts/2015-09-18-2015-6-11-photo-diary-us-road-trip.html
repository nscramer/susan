A little over a year ago, myself along with the two other members of my team at work got to go on one crazy, exhausting, exhilarating, wonderful road trip across the US.

Without getting into too much detail, we directly build and nurture relationships with content creators who use our online tools. The goal of this trip was to meet these content creators, IRL, and spend some time getting to know them. We took a poll and found the cities which were the most populated by our Ahalogy family and took off!

With meet-ups each night in a different city, we hit 9 cities in 10 days, starting in NYC and ending in San Fransisco.
Along the way we stopped in: NYC, Philly, D.C., Atlanta, Chicago, Kansas City, Denver, Salt Lake City, and San Fransisco.

Yes, that means we spent roughly 12 hours each day in the car, high-tailing it to the next city. While it was absolutely insane and there were days I thought I’d rather just hop a plane home out of pure exhaustion, it was one of the best things I’ve ever done. I saw so much of this amazing country that I wouldn’t have seen otherwise, and I met people face to face who I’d previously only been able to talk to through a computer screen.
