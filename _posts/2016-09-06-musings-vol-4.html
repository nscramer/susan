Hello friends! 
If you're in the US, I hope you had a fabulous holiday weekend. And if you're in the Mid-West, I hope you spent most of it outside soaking up this glorious weather we've been having. 
To start this short week off right, I have another set of musings for you! 

READING: 

Just finished: A re-read of Is Everyone Hanging Out Without Me by Mindy Kaling. Did you catch my Instagram post about this? (ps- if you're not following, get to it!) Sometimes, when I'm in an off-mood, I turn to books I've read and that brought me joy. I've also read Why Not Me? by Mindy, but this specific rut called for her first book. I love her story and her witty sense of humor, it leaves me "lolling" for hours. 

Just started: Good Morning, Midnight. I don't have much to say yet about this one as I'm only on page 16. What drew me to it was—let's be honest—the cover showcasing a beautiful starry sky. Upon reading the synopsis, it seemed to have a similar feel as The Martian, which is a favorite of mine. My mom ended up picking up this new book, and passed it along to me after finishing. Can't wait to see how it unfolds! 

LISTENING:

Anderson Paak: I'm not sure I can do him justice with any description I come up with. I really dig his sound. It's pretty different to most things I listen to, and we've been playing him over the speakers in the house quite a bit. Thanks to Nick, for this music discovery. 

Same Drugs- MisterWives: This cover of a song I already enjoyed—has been repeated many, many times in my headphones the last couple of weeks. I can't wait for more music from MisterWives. They are sure to cure any bad mood you come across. 

TRYING:

Keto Double Chocolate Brownies: We recently got a ketogenic cookbook, Bacon & Butter as a gift, and I've been cooking almost exclusively from it's arsenal of yummy recipes. We've loved everything we've made so far, so I can't wait to try this special treat! 

SWOONING: This amazing kitchen (smeg fridge dreams), Japanese inspired plateware, this camera bag, and these cute-as-can-be pineapple donuts made by my good friend (and amazing baker/blogger), Sarah! 

Are you reading, listening, trying or swooning? Let's chat about it!
