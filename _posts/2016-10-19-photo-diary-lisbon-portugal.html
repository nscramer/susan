Oh, Lisboa. You magical city, you. This city captivates you with it's bright colors and blue, blue skies. At the same time, it pummels you with it's hills ;) 
We were in Lisbon for about 9 days, and I could have spent 9 more! From the delicious food to the breathtaking views, this city has so much to offer. It was so fun to explore and behold all of it's glory. Not to mention, Lisbon is a very affordable city, which is a major plus. I hope you enjoy looking through the photos, it was so hard to narrow down which ones would go in this post! Expect to see some more detailed posts about what to do and see in Lisbon in future posts.
