I don’t know about you, but lately I’m really getting the duo-chrome vibes. I’ve always loved opal, holographic things, and other pretty light-reflecting items. 

When it comes to duo-chrome, it’s having a big moment in makeup. See: Urban Decay Moondust palette, Makeup Geek Duo-Chrome shadows, the Kat Von D Transformer Liquid Lipstick, the list goes on. 

I’ve purchased some makeup items in the past, but haven’t really gotten comfortable wearing them in many looks. One place I have found to let my duo-chrome flag fly, however, is my nails! 

I have an obscene amount of nail colors like this, considering the amount I actually wear, but we’ll look past that.  I have a few all-time favorites in my collection: OPI Breathe Life, Essie Nude Beach, and H&M Science Fiction. 

OPI Breath Life was a limited edition release many years ago, but you can still find it on Amazon! It’s the most traditionally opalescent. It’s base is a pearly white with blue and green-ish shifts. It’s beautiful. The perfect color for a wedding shower or heck, even the big day! This is a go-to for me on my nails since it’s subtle but still gives off a little something. The proceeds from this little guy went (and probably still go) to lung cancer research, which hits close to home for my family and makes it a little more special. 

Essie Nude Beach is similar to Breath Life in that it’s quite sheer, but it’s a baby pink with a pale blue shift and sometimes even looks a bit orange. It’s a newer find of mine, but fits right into the realm of what I love to wear on my nails on an everyday basis. 

H&M Science Fiction. This one is, quite obviously, a bit farther out of the box for me—but would ya look at it? What a beaut. I will say, when applied to the nails it pulls very green, and you lose a bit of that deep blue that you see in the bottle. Still, it’s a dream, right?
