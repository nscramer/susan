As promised, a photo diary from my birthday celebrations! 

This is a real look into how I celebrated my birthday this year. Nothing is staged, although I did force my friends to pose for a few pictures for me :). My original plan was to have a more artsy-style photo diary, but then I realized to get those types of shots, the photographer would have to be an observer, rather than a participant. And I wanted everyone in attendance to be participating! So, we grabbed an Instax camera and captured some shots, which I'm sharing with you edit-free. Here's a look into real life for me: we invited some of my great friends over to our home, had some (a lot) of drinks, and it was a blast. Yes, I drink good beer and cheap beer. Yes, we played a drinking game. Yes, you'll see lots of bug spray because mosquitos eat the sh*t out of me. Yes, we are goofballs. And yes, it was a birthday for the books. Thanks to everyone who celebrated and sent well wishes!
