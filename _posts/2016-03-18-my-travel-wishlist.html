Morocco— I’ve wanted to go to africa for a long time now, and morocco always caught my eye. The colorful culture, great food, and warm temps seem like my kinda place. With all of the markets to explore and a wildly different cultural experience than anywhere I've been, it's high on my list! 

Image via The Fresh Exchange

Portugal— this is a tiny bit of a cheat, because Nick and I are planning to go there this year. We’re looking to go to Lisbon for around 7-9 days, and I couldn’t be more excited! The beautiful colors and breathtaking architecture are definitely a draw. I’ve also never been to a country that speaks portugese…nor do I know a single word… so that should be fun! I’m excited to learn some conversational phrases and explore the beautiful, water-side town.

Image via Tuula

Italy— is there anyone out there who doesn’t want to visit Italy? I mean, hello, PASTA. Right? Right. And besides the pasta, it’s just seemingly an all around beautiful place. I’d definitely like to spend time in all the usual suspects of Italy.

Image via Conde Nast Traveller

Ireland—This is partly influenced by the fact that i’m part Irish, and partly by my love of cooler, coastal places that aren’t necessarily a spring break destination. My brother recently spent three weeks in the UK and had wonderful things to say about Ireland, which only feeds my fire more.
