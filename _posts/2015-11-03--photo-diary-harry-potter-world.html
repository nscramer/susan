For years, my mom & I have been dreaming of going to The Wizarding World of Harry Potter at Universal Studios. I grew up reading these books and my mom always read them with me. Booking the trip was pretty surreal and i'm so happy we went! They really did an amazing job with both of the parks. I can't even pick my favorite between the two, it was everything I hoped it would be & more! 

We were able to do all the Harry Potter things in one day, and with our extra day in Orlando we even drove over to Cocoa Beach for a little vitamin sea.
