Guys. We're already halfway through September. WTH?? There's only two-ish weeks till the Portugal trip—so this past weekend I got into hard-core planning mode to get content all ready to go and pre-scheduled for while we're gone! Don't forget to follow me on Instagram, as it's probably the only form of social media I'll be using while we are in Portugal. 

While I was organizing what posts to write and photos to shoot, I realized I've been pretty exclusively using a set of products for a solid 3 weeks now. I do try new things quite often, but I haven't been able to put these products down! Grab a snack and a cup of tea—there's quite a bit to talk about. 

I'll go in order of application, excluding base. I've got a whole post coming up soon on my current routine for my makeup base + what products I've been using religiously! The first thing I do after my base is brows. To be honest, I pretty much consider them part of the base since I can't leave the house without doing something to my brows. I went to Ulta a few weeks back to pick up another tube of my tried and true brow gel, the Clear Brow Gel from Anastasia of Beverly Hills, and saw they had this fun kit which included that gel, a dual-ended brush, and their brow powder all for $36 and I couldn't pass it up. The brow gel was full size in the kit and for reference, on it's own it costs $22. I do love a good deal, especially when it's on products I am repurchasing! I got my normal shade, Taupe, thinking I'd just use the powder here and there since I've been loving the Colourpop brow products. However it's become a daily staple! Having two shades really helps build natural looking brows, and the brush is pretty small at first look, but I'm obsessed! 

From there we'll go to the cheeks, where I've been using the Lorac Color Source Blush in Spectra. This shade is your typical pink color, a usual shade in my blush rotations. I love how creamy this powder is. It's pretty pigmented but builds beautifully. I also went digging through my sample stash recently and came across the cutest little sample of Balm Desert by The Balm. I've been using this as a blush/bronzer/contour shade. It has a pretty sheen to it and the color works so well just alone on the cheeks. 

Next stop is the eyes. While I haven't been wearing this eyeshadow everyday, I have been using it more than you'd think considering the color. This is the Urban Decay eyeshadow in Tonic. It's an amazing duo-chrome shadow that's lavender with pink + blue reflects. I picked this up a long time ago but couldn't really figure out how to wear it until recently. I love this on the inner corner for a bit of intrigue or smudged along the lower lash line. It gives such and interesting twist to an everyday look. Plus, it fits right in with the moon child/fairy thing that's a big trend right now—which I'm totally into. For the lashes I've been coating them in the new offering from L'Oreal: the Voluminous Feline. I'm not sure this is going to become a staple for me after this tube is gone, but it's definitely great for some volume and length that isn't clumpy! 

Lastly, a new (to me) lippie I can't put down is the Maybeline Color Sensational 'The Buffs' Lipstick in Sin-a-mon. This browny-grey nude gives the most beautiful wash of color and isn't too glossy. It's a color you don't even need a mirror to apply and will go with every look—it's earned it's home in my handbag. 

Phew, that's it! Have you tried any of these items? What are you loving right now?
