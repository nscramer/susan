This past weekend was the Cincinnati Flower Show here in town, so Nick and I went to have a look and get some great photos of one of my favorite things about this earth: flowers! 

If you’ve never heard of it before, the Cincinnati Flower Show is put on by the Cincinnati Horticultural Society each year and this year they partnered with the Cincinnati USA Sister City Association to put on a spectacular show of exhibits and vendors! People come in from all over the world to display their work and sell their plants and flowers. You can find vendors selling plants, unique flowers, and even food + drink. 

There were also exhibits of beautiful flower arrangements and gardening. We really loved our time there!
