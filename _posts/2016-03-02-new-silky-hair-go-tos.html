As I’ve talked about before, not too long ago I put my hair through some things. Lots of processing which completely dried it out worse than I’ve experienced before. I even had about four inches lopped off to take care of some of the damage. Now, this was entirely my own fault because I wanted that sleek grey hair! And I got it. And I loved it. For a short time :)

Since then, i’ve been on the hunt for the right products to repair my hair without overloading it. Before I went grey, my hair was actually quite limp, lifeless, and got greasy quite quickly. Post-grey, it's completely different. I'd still call it lifeless, but also dry as can be. 

So, the new (ish) L’Oreal Extraordinary Oil range is a great option for me! I’ve been testing out the shampoo, conditioner, and serum for about a week, and I am overall pretty pleased with these products! 

First off, they smell so good. Secondly, they really hydrate and replenish my hair without causing any buildup or greasiness. 

The Lustrous Oil Serum is by far my favorite item from the range, as hair serums/oils are a staple product for me. If you do a lot of heat styling with your hair—you’ve got to start using oils, they’ll change your hair! 

This oil has helped a ton with the ends of my hair, I apply a hefty amount to the ends before blow-drying, and any excess left on my hands i’ll run lightly through the roots. My hair would be in complete shambles without a product like this. 

The conditioner comes in second for me; as I see it as a great conditioner but it didn’t really wow me. I am really finicky with conditioners, and honestly tend to use masks a lot instead. But I will definitely be using this up as my regular conditioner option! 

And in third place from what i’ve tried, the shampoo. This really isn’t to do with the product, I think it leaves my hair feeling clean and soft. However, I do have very cool-toned, ashy blonde hair, so I prefer to use a deep violet toning shampoo to keep any and all warmth from creeping into my locks. 

Overall, if you’re struggling with dry, hard to manage hair—I think this range will be great for you! The smell is great, and you’ll have silky hair in no time!
