At the beginning of this year, nick and I didn’t have any travel planned, but we knew we wanted to check off another destination on our list and experience something new. When it came down to picking, it was mid May, and I was really into going to a tropical destination. It didn’t take long to be drawn to the Caribbean. We settled on Saint Martin quickly, as it’s a French speaking island! (well, half of it). 

We had an amazing time, virtually had the beach to ourselves each day, exploring, and eating amazing food.
