Reading:
Just finished: Good Morning, Midnight
I really liked this book. First of all, it's about space, the sky, starts, and not taking your life for granted. So, I think we could all have assumed I'd enjoy it. I even didn't mind a total clif-hanger ending! Highly recommend. (if you liked The Martain, you'll love it!)

Just started: Stardust
This is my bookclub's choice for this month. I've been wanting to get into Neil Gaiman forever, so I'm excited to finally be starting! Will definitely report back on this one as I've been told I will enjoy it. I'm also tackling what's on my Vacation Reading List!

Listening:
Relax + Unwind Playlist: This Spotify playlist is what I've been tuning to whenever I have a shooting day. I tend to shoot all of my photos on either Saturday or Sunday mornings, when I can get the best lighting. This often results in multiple hours of photo taking for many different posts in one session, so having the right music is key. This playlist gives me the right amount of uplifting energy without stressin' me out. (does anyone else have trouble concentrating with super fast-paced tunes?) 
Dave Matthews Band: an age-old favorite of mine. Lately I've strayed from listening to all their musical wonder in the same capacity I used to, but I always come back. I have a playlist made on Spotify which has all of their songs (that are available on there), and I've been selecting it often!
