Weekly musings is back! I drummed up this idea for a weekly series and then it fell off my radar, but i'm bringing it back! I really love reading these types of posts on other blogs, because I love to get suggestions on things I should check out next. I've broken this up into four categories, and i'm going to share some of my favorite things in each of them weekly. I hope you find something you like! 

Also, if there's a category you'd like to see that's not here—let a gal know! 

Reading

This book takes you through the history and art of oil making, and how it’s been taken advantage of in recent times. It’s very enlightening if you’re a foodie!

Strangely, I have not read this! I just received a beautiful copy from a friend for my birthday, and I dove right in.
